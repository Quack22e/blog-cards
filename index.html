DJI's Origins

DJI, founded by Frank Wang in 2006, started with flight controllers for model airplanes. It gained prominence with the release of the DJI Phantom in 2013, a consumer drone featuring a stabilized camera. Since then, DJI has become a global leader in civilian drones, renowned for innovation and user-friendly designs, shaping the industry's future.
